The Random forest performs FAR better than logistic regression, so we use the feature importances attribute of the random forest to see the most important factors deciding whether or not someone clicked on the card. The most significant features are:

1.) Female Gender

2.) affluency

3.) Male Gender

4.) Living in the Greater London area

You can see that the large amount of counties in the data seemed to 'confuse' the logistic regression, whereas the decision tree is easily able to 'see through' the swath of counties and find the truly important features
